Equilibria 2018

BIOPRODUCT DESIGN, DEVELOPMENT AND MANAGEMENT

The third installment aims at bringing together the innovative ideas of young individuals in the field of product designing and entrepreneuship. We provide the ideal platform for presenting newly designed or developed bioproducts and related technological processes, that assure the enhancement of science and economic development. This theme has been expanded to bring together all engineering and bio-science streams, capable of contributing to bioproduct development; be it designing models, algorithms, software, or instruments. Equilibria 18 also caters on effective intercommunication between students and established entrepreneurs, regarding the initiatory link between designing and developing bioproducts, intending to encourage the development of modern sciences, and creating technological correction, Equilibria 2018 is an inceptive for associating erudition with the economic biz.
