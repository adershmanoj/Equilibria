ABOUT EVOGEN

The young association of biotechnology department students of SCTCE was established in 2014, with the basic objective of empowering students of biotechnology, through creation of a conducive environment for them to exress their basic basic skill sets as well as to aid them in developing new skills which could help them in fullfilling their commitment towards the society by pursuing a fullfilling career in the area of Biotechnology and to aid them in spreading the spirit of Biotechnology so as to take this venerable discipline to newer heights in the days ahead.

Under EVOGEN, we were able to successfully conduct National Science Day Celebrations, Ozone Celebrations, etc by arranging events like bio quizzes, poster presentation, expert lectures and workshops using the fund sanctioned by Kerala State Council for Science Technology and Environment (KSCTSE), CMFRI, Bharat Petroleum, Indian Oil. We were also able to organise a National level symposium Equilibria 2016 on Advances in Biosciences & Bioengineering and Equilibria 2017 on Trends & prospects in Bio-Science and Technology towards Sustainability.

Equilibria

BIOPRODUCT DESIGN, DEVELOPMENT AND MANAGEMENT

The third installment aims at bringing together the innovative ideas of young individuals in the field of product designing and entrepreneuship. We provide the ideal platform for presenting newly designed or developed bioproducts and related technological processes, that assure the enhancement of science and economic development. This theme has been expanded to bring together all engineering and bio-science streams, capable of contributing to bioproduct development; be it designing models, algorithms, software, or instruments. Equilibria 18 also caters on effective intercommunication between students and established entrepreneurs, regarding the initiatory link between designing and developing bioproducts, intending to encourage the development of modern sciences, and creating technological correction, Equilibria 2018 is an inceptive for associating erudition with the economic biz.

INSTITUTION

SCT College of Engineering

Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering (SCTCE), Trivandrum, was established by the Government of Kerala in the year 1995 in memoriam of the Great Maharaja of Travancore and is affiliated to the University of Kerala with AICTE approval. The college is a realisation of a long felt need for an institution catering to a growing number of undergraduates opting for technical education.
It seeks to promote education and research in the field of technology; to accumulate effective ideas and techniques likely to promote the socio-economic welfare of the people of Kerala and be able to provide proper guidance and support to young men and women who can make these ideas work.
The management commitee is lead by the Honorable Minister for Transport and it's eminent membership includes representatives of Government of Kerala viz: -the Higher Education Secretary, Finance Secretary, Transport Secretary, Managing Director of Kerala State Road Transport Corporation.

DEPARTMENT

DEPARTMENT OF BIOTECHNOLOGY & BIOCHEMICAL ENGINEERING

Established in the year 2002, the Department of Biotechnology & Biochemical Engineering is the youngest at Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering. Over the past decade, the department has been offering an undergraduate (B.Tech) programme in Biotechnology and Biochemical Engineering. The faculty of this department constitutes a unique pool with expertise in diverse subject areas like Microbiology, Biochemistry, Biotechnology and Chemical Engineering. The department has been constantly maintaining the mindset of aligning it's resources towards the welfare of the society. In the past, it has organised several state of the art training programmes, workshops, seminars and conferences under the aegis of the TEQIP scheme (Government of India), for the benifit of faculty, students and the general public.
