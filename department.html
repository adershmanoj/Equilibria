DEPARTMENT OF BIOTECHNOLOGY & BIOCHEMICAL ENGINEERING

Established in the year 2002, the Department of Biotechnology & Biochemical Engineering is the youngest at Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering. Over the past decade, the department has been offering an undergraduate (B.Tech) programme in Biotechnology and Biochemical Engineering. The faculty of this department constitutes a unique pool with expertise in diverse subject areas like Microbiology, Biochemistry, Biotechnology and Chemical Engineering.
The department has been constantly maintaining the mindset of aligning it's resources towards the welfare of the society. In the past, it has organised several state of the art training programmes, workshops, seminars and conferences under the aegis of the TEQIP scheme (Government of India), for the benifit of faculty, students and the general public. These include:

  • in silico Biology - A course cum workshop (2005)
  • Bioinfo-Blitz - A workshop on Bioinformatics (2007)
  • Faculty development progrramme in Environmental Biotechnology (2008)
  • Faculty development programme on Advances in Nanotechnology (2009)
  • Community development programme on food production (2009)
  • Students' training programme on Bioprocess engineering (2009)
  • National conference on Advances Processing Technologies (2010)
