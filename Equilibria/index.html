About Us

The National Symposium EQUILIBRIA conducted by the association of biotechnology students of Department of Biotechnology & Biochemical Engineering at Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering, EVOGEN, has been conducted annually for the past 2 years. The theme for this third edition is Bio-entrepreneurship: Bioproduct design, Development and Management. Sustainable and economic development of the world lies in the hands of modern science. Maximum exploration of the secrets of the biological world is subordinate considering the unsolved problems, owing to the lack of properly established technology. In the field of Biotechnology & Biochemical Engineering, several new ideas are composed all over the world, but very little of them are popularised socially or publicly disclosed. Bio-entrepreneurship is the process of creating value from life science innovation. Considering the current economic trend of biotechnology in economic field, the world is in need of many more entrepreneurs capable of uplifting the innovations created by academicians and other professionals.

EQUILIBRIA 2017

After the successful completion of Equilibria 2016, the second edition was held on April 2017 with the motto - evolution via innovation. It proved to be a brilliant platform for students, academicians, entrepreneurs and research scholars to step into a world of scientific research through sustainable development. Active participation was witnessed with more than 200 presentations from all over India.Research articles covering almost all areas of advances in biosciences and bioengineering were presented and amongst them, the most relevant and innovative ones were honored with the BEST PAPER and BEST POSTER AWARD. The event was also flouished by experts from research and academia, addressing current developments and emerging trends. Amongst other technical events conducted, a demonstration class on Kung Fu was also organised.

EQUILIBRIA 2016

Equilibria 2016 was a maiden attempt to pool intellectual resources from all relevant domains of bioscience research, with almost more than 200 participants from more than 30 institutions across South India. Research articles covering up almost all areas of advances in biosciences and bioengineering were presented and amongst them, the most relevant and innovative ones were honored with the BEST PAPER and BEST POSTER AWARD. Lectures related to the topic were dealt by scientists, entrepreneurs and academicians from the most prestigious national research institutes like CSIR, CTCRI, TBGRI etc. Apart from explorations to wide range of scientific avenues, Equilibria 2016 also brought the traditional cultural fiesta of Kerala into limelight.
